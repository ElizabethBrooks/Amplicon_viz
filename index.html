Amplicon Explorer

16S / ITS Diversity Analysis
Comprehensive exploratory analysis of taxonomic diversity and community composition. Start here to understand your microbiome data through alpha/beta diversity, taxa composition, and core microbiome analysis.
  • Alpha Diversity (Shannon, Simpson, Richness)
  • Beta Ordination (NMDS, PCoA)
  • Taxa Composition Stacked Bar Charts
  • Hierarchical Clustering
  • PERMANOVA Testing
  • Core Microbiome Analysis
  • Rarefaction Curves

Functional Profiler

DESeq2 & PICRUSt2 Analysis
Specialized app for differential abundance analysis and metabolic pathway prediction. Use after Amplicon Explorer to understand which specific taxa and pathways drive community differences.
  • DESeq2 Differential Abundance
  • Volcano Plots & Statistics
  • PICRUSt2 Pathway Analysis
  • Multiple DAA Methods (LinDA, ALDEx2, DESeq2, edgeR)
  • KO / MetaCyc / EC Pathways
  • PCA & Heatmap Visualization
  • KO-to-KEGG Annotation
